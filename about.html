About Us

Serving our community with compassion, dignity, and professional excellence since 2016. Our mission is to provide quality funeral services that honor every life, regardless of economic circumstances.

Our Founder's Vision

Chosen Generation Funeral Directors was established in 2016 by Mr. Siyabonga Mogiba. After gaining three years of valuable experience in the funeral industry, he recognized a critical need in our community.

Mr. Mogiba saw the importance of reaching out not just to the affluent, but also to the disadvantaged. He understood that many people are the sole breadwinners in their families and simply cannot afford the high costs of traditional funeral cover.

"Our mission is to ensure that every family, regardless of their economic circumstances, can honor their loved ones with dignity and respect."

- Mr. Siyabonga Mogiba, Founder

Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in funeral services

"

The primary function of our business is the internal securing and protection of our clients' assets. Due to the high inflation prices in the funeral industry in our country, funeral policies have become a lucrative business.

This however has opened the door for businesses to be exploited by various substandard or dishonest funeral companies, making the securing of clients' money more of a liability than an asset.

Our clients can be assured of our full dedication and professional service, placing their best interests first at all times.
